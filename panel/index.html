02 Opening

Panel Norms

Every session opens with these commitments. Read each one aloud as a panel and have each member confirm before proceeding.

🤝 Panel Commitments — Check Each One
Confidentiality. Nothing discussed in this room leaves this room. What we hear here, stays here.
Curiosity, not interrogation. Our questions are meant to help the student process — not to put them on trial.
Empathy before judgment. We seek to understand before we recommend.
Fairness and connection. Our recommendations must be related to the harm, respectful in tone, and realistic to complete.
Bias check. If any panel member has a direct connection to this case, they will step back and an alternate will serve.
Check all norms to continue.
